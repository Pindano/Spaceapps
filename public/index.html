The Impact of Deforestation

Climate Change

Deforestation contributes to climate change by releasing stored carbon into the atmosphere and reducing the Earth's capacity to absorb CO2.

Biodiversity Loss

Forests are home to countless species. Their destruction leads to habitat loss and threatens the survival of many plants and animals.

Soil Erosion

Without tree roots to hold soil in place, deforestation increases erosion, leading to landslides and reduced soil fertility.

Water Cycle Disruption

Forests play a crucial role in the water cycle. Their loss can lead to altered rainfall patterns and increased flooding.
